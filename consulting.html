WE PROVIDE PEER CONSULTING

Each day, we have student consultants available to talk about anything Data Science. These students love talking to you about Data Science!

Are you just getting started in Data Science? Do you want to learn more about opportunities available on campus? Or do you simply want to chat with someone about Data Science? Come talk to our consultants.

Check the calendar below and look at each of our consultants for their expertise. We look forward to seeing you!

OUR PARTNERSHIP

In collaboration with Moffitt Undergraduate Library, the Berkeley Division of Data Science is teaming up with campus organizations such as DS course staff, student clubs, and DS research groups in order to provide a hub for all students interested in exploring data science at UC Berkeley.
